About All West Indies Pickleball Association (AWIPA)

The All West Indies Pickleball Association (AWIPA) is dedicated to promoting and developing pickleball throughout the West Indies. Our mission is to foster a dynamic community where players of all ages and skill levels can enjoy, learn, and compete in this exciting sport.

OUR
MISSION

At AWIPA, our mission is to:
- Foster the growth of pickleball throughout the West Indies.
- Provide high-quality training and resources for players, coaches, and officials.
- Organize local, regional, and national tournaments to encourage competitive play.
- Promote health, fitness, and fun through pickleball.
